King Birendra

A man with billion hearts

King Birendra, on the right, interacting with a Nepalese soldier.

Here is a time line for Late King Birendra's life:

  • 1945 - born at Narayanhiti Royal Palace, Kathmandu, Nepal

  • 1955 - after his grandfather Late King tribhuvan died and his father king mahendra ascendes the throne he became the crown prince of nepal

  • 1959 - Enrolls in Eton college in the UK

  • 1964 - Returns back to Nepal

  • 1967 - spends some time studying in university of tokyo

  • 1967-1968 - studies political theory at Harvard University

  • 1970 - marries to Aishwarya Rajya Laxmi Devi, his second cousin

  • 1972 - Ascends the Nepalese Throne, he was an absolute monarch

  • 1973 - visits India on October and China two months later to maintain good relation with these countries as Nepal is sandwiched between these two absolute asian powers

  • 1975 - holds his coronation, which was attended by politicians and political leaders from 60 nations

  • 1990 - a popular prodemocracy movement led to demonstrations that erupted into bloody clashes between the soldiers and police and the demonstrators. Due to which he lifts the ban on political parties and promulgates the new constitution ,which transforms Nepal into a constitutional monarchy

  • 2001 - Dipendra(king Birendra's son) guns down king Birendra and the entire royal family as Birendra disagreed with his choice of bride
